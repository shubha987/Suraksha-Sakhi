Rights of Women in Indian Constitution

Here are some of the key rights granted to women in the Indian Constitution:

  • Right to Equality: Women are guaranteed equal rights and opportunities in all spheres of life.
  • Right to Freedom: Women have the right to freedom of speech, expression, and movement.
  • Right to Education: Women have the right to education without any discrimination.
  • Right to Work: Women have the right to work and choose their profession.
  • Right to Property: Women have the right to own and inherit property.
  • Right to Protection: Women have the right to protection against violence, harassment, and discrimination.
  • Right to Health: Women have the right to access healthcare services and reproductive rights.
  • Right to Participation: Women have the right to participate in political, social, and economic activities.
